Hyginus Memorial College (C.H.M.C) has established itself as a bastion of academic excellence and character development. This esteemed institution is dedicated to fostering a supportive and inclusive community of learners, where students can grow, explore, and thrive. Mission At C.H.M.C, we are committed to providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in all aspects of life. Our mission is to inspire young minds, cultivate their talents, and empower them to become responsible, compassionate, and visionary leaders. Academic Excellence With a team of dedicated and experienced educators, C.H.M.C offers a diverse range of subjects and courses, carefully designed to meet the needs of our students. Our state-of-the-art facilities and resources create a conducive learning environment, encouraging students to explore, innovate, and achieve their full potential.
